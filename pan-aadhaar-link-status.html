Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

PAN Aadhaar link status kaise check karein?

Income Tax Department ki official website par jaakar PAN aur Aadhaar number enter karke link status check kiya ja sakta hai.

Agar PAN Aadhaar se link nahi hai to kya hoga?

Agar PAN Aadhaar se link nahi hota hai to PAN inoperative ho sakta hai aur income tax related kaam nahi ho payenge.

PAN Aadhaar link karne ki last date kya hai?

Government dwara nirdharit last date tak PAN Aadhaar link karna anivarya hota hai. Late linking par penalty lag sakti hai.

PAN Aadhaar link karne ka official website kaunsa hai?

Income Tax Department ki official website https://www.incometax.gov.in par PAN Aadhaar linking ki suvidha di jati hai.
